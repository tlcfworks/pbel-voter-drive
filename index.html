Voter ID Update & Registration Drive

This is more than a campaign; it's a movement to put PBEL City on the map. Updating your Voter ID isn’t just paperwork - it’s power.

The Problem?

Our collective inaction has led to significant civic challenges

Absence of Local Visibility - Most residents still vote in other constituencies, making PBEL City invisible in local electoral data and planning.

Broken Infrastructure Promises - From pothole ridden roads to unreliable public utilities, civic projects bypass our community due to lack of voter pressure.

Community Voice Without Collective Weight - We raise issues on forums, but without electoral backing, our concerns carry little weight with local authorities.

Delayed Civic Development - Projects like street lighting, lake preservation, and footpaths are deprioritized because PBEL is not seen as a unified electoral bloc.

A future where PBEL City sets the benchmark for civic power.

Carry These Documents

Please bring self-attested soft-copies.

Address Proof

(For Address Update & New Voter ID)

  • Rent Agreement
  • Sales Deed / Property Tax
  • Bank/Post Office Passbook

Relationship Proof

(If Address Proof is not in your name)

  • Birth / Marriage Certificate
  • Passport / Aadhaar with related person's name

ID & Date of Birth Proof

(For New Voter ID)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Birth/Class X/XII Certificate

Photograph

(For New Voter ID Only)

  • Passport size color photo
  • With a white background
